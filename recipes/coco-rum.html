Coco Rum Punch

Description

This is one of my favourite cocktails. It was introduced to to me by NikiB . In my brother's wedding, and it was a delicious, fruity, and sweet surprise.

Ingredients:

Fortunately I do know the ingredients for this recipe, so here they go:

  • 1 bottle of Malibu coco rum;
  • 1 bottle of orange juice;
  • Ice.

Steps:

Steps for this cocktail should be relatively straightforward:

  1. Add 50 ml of Malibu coco rum to a cup;
  2. Add 100 ml of orange juice to the cup;
  3. Add ice to cool down (maybe the ice should have been added before the liquids).
  4. Enjoy!
