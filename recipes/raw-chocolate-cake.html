Raw Chocorange Cake

Description

This Raw Chocolate Cake is a vegan, no-oven cake. It's a thick, moist chocolate cake with a delicious citric taste.

It was discovered by Nicole Baudo on an all vegan indian food (or something like that) course.

Ingredients:

  • 120 ml Unsweetened Coco Milk;
  • 3 tbsp. of Honey;
  • 120 grams of Cacao;
  • 1,5 tbsp. of Coco oil;
  • 1,5 Avocados;
  • 3 tbsp. of Lemon Juice;
  • 1 cup of Seedless raisins;
  • 0,2 cups of Sunflower Seeds;
  • 0,6 cups of nuts.
